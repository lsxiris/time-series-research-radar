# Data Sources

## Current Public Source Types

- OpenReview conference and forum pages
- PMLR proceedings pages
- Official conference websites
- DOI landing pages
- Public PDF links

## Current Coverage

### ICLR 2026

- Public-source pack for 14 time-series related papers
- OpenReview thread files in raw and structured forms

### ICML 2025

- Time-series related papers filtered from PMLR Volume 267
- Titles, paper-page links, PDF links, OpenReview links, abstracts, and keywords

### KDD 2025

- Time-series related papers collected from public KDD 2025 paper lists
- Research, ADS, and benchmark-track coverage
- Titles, DOI links, track labels, and keywords

## Known Limitations

- KDD 2025 abstract extraction is incomplete because ACM DL pages are protected by anti-bot checks
- NeurIPS 2025 coverage is still pending because the checked proceedings path did not expose a stable 2025 volume at collection time
- Local PDF downloads are intentionally excluded from Git tracking under `local_papers/`

## Repository Policy

This repository stores links, structured metadata, and generated public outputs. It does not include private annotations, private research notes, or unpublished experimental material.
